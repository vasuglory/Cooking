Ingredients:
brown bread - 2 slices
onions - 1
tomatoes - 1
chilli - 1
salt

Process:
lit the stove, add pan
add 1 spoon of ghee
add onions
add tomatoes
add chilli
add prawns
add salt
  fry till onions and prawns become slightly soft
  take them and keep them aside.

  take slice bread apply ghee and add the fried stuff and between the two slices.
add stuffed bread on to the pan
add some ghee
  roast for max 1min on each side

  SERVE IT
